## Support Mori

For now, the best ways to support Mori:

- **Star the repo** on [GitHub](https://github.com/psrth/mori) — it helps with visibility and signals that people find this useful
- **Share your experience** — tell us what worked, what didn't, and what you'd like to see next by [opening an issue](https://github.com/psrth/mori/issues)
- **Share with other developers** — if Mori helped you, tell a friend or mention it in your community

That support means the world. Building a database proxy that works across 9 engines and 14 providers is a large undertaking, and knowing people find it useful is what keeps the project moving forward.
