## Support Mori

For now, the best ways to support Mori are just by helping me discover edge cases!

- **Star the repo** on [GitHub](https://github.com/psrth/mori) — the more people that use mori, the more edge cases we can discover and fix!
- **Share your experience** — tell us what worked, what didn't, and what you'd like to see next by [opening an issue](https://github.com/psrth/mori/issues)
- **Share with other developers** — if Mori helped you, tell a friend or mention it in your community
- For anything else, feel free to reach out to me at [mori@psrth.sh](mailto:mori@psrth.sh).


### PS:
If you're OpenAI and want to acquire this repository, that is always welcome.
